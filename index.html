See the projects danielh@wholeyield.com

What this is

A one-person studio, not an agency and not a startup looking for a round.

Daniel builds and ships the software himself, drawing on a background in enterprise conversational AI and cloud architecture, plus years of running a small business before this one.

The work is deliberately narrow. Few projects, carried a long way, rather than many carried a little.

The name is the intent: take a thing all the way to harvest rather than abandoning it half grown.
